Hello, Darlings!

June 6th

What have you been up to? No, don’t tell me - I don’t really care, haha.

All I care about today is SHOES. Shoes are hats for your feet. Meaning, if you put them on, they better be good, giiiiirl. Now, there are two kinds of shoes: walking shoes and talking shoes. If your Uber is stuck or the weather’s good or something and you need to get from point A to point B, put on those walkers and walk, baby. Nothing’s wrong with that. Nothing’s right, too, but hey - it’s your life.

If you want to make an impression, you better wear talking shoes. They are called like that because when some tall dark and gorgeous sees you in them, he won’t be able to shut up about it. “My God, my Zeus, my mama and papa these are some nice beautiful shoes,” he might say. Don’t you melt then and there, girl. Say, “Yah, I know.” AND WALK AWAY.

Now, killer shoes don’t have to be high-heel stilettos, killer shoes don’t mean they kill your feet, babe. No, all they have to do is make an impact. Pick a pair that’s close to your heart and wear a dress that does not distract from the main attraction. No, the main attraction is not your shoes, it’s your SOUL, girl.
